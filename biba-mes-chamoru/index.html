Biba Mes Chamoru!

Biba Chamorro Month!

By David Taitingfong | March 01, 2021 | 2 minutes
todu chamoru

Håfa adai todus hamyu guennao huyung! På’gu na ha’åni i tinituhun-ña nu Mes Chamoru! Hu tungu' na ti sessu mångge' yu' guini, lao hu prometi hamyu na hu chachagi! Kada diha gof tinani' ha' yu': guaha mampus che’cho'-hu, guaha mampus tarehan guma' put eskuela, ya manhåhalom ha' ham yan i manggafå-hu gi un nuebun guma'.

Ai adai, fannå’i yu' i animås-miyu fan.

Siempri bai kefångge' guini, kolo’lo’ña sa' på’gu i Mes Chamoru. Fanhasso ha' todus hamyu: sigi ha' u’usa i fino'-ta; sigi ha' ume’eyak yan tumutungu' todu siña hamyu. Mungnga chumathinasso put linachi gi tinigi'-miyu pat sinångan-miyu. Todu maolek ha'. Sigi ha' chachagi.

Biba Mes Chamoru! Na’lå’la' todu kosas Chamoru!

Todu ha' para på’gu. Bula guinaiya! — DJT.

English Translation

Hello to everyone out there! Today is the beginning of Chamorro Month! I know that I don’t often write write here, but I promise you I’m trying! It’s just every day I’ve been so busy: too much work, too much school homework, and we’re still moving into a new house.

Ai adai, please lend me your strength.

I’ll definitely try to write here, especially because it’s Chamorro Month. Just remember everyone: keep using the language; keep learning all that you can. Don’t worry about errors in your writing or your words. It’s all good. Just keep trying.

Biba Chamorro Month! Celebrate all things Chamorro!

That’s all for now. Much love! — DJT.
