Dididi' nu Guåhu


Håfa adai! I na’ån-hu si David Taitingfong låo matungo'-ha as Dabit. Hu fa’tinas ini na blog sa' malagu' yu' fanugi' nu i hinanao-hu umetyak finu' Chamoru. Ginin atyu, ma’a’gu dididi' ini na blog. Gi tinituhun, hu tútugi' nu i håfa inetyåk-ku, låo manugi' ha' yu' maseha håfa på’gu låo gi finu' Chamoru!

Mohon na sénmaolik i bisitå-mu guini!

Pinila'

Hello! My name is David Taitingfong but I go by Dabit. I made this blog because I wanted to write about my journey to learn the Chamorro language. It has changed a bit since then. Previously I was writing about whatever I learned, but now I just write about whatever but in Chamorro.

I hope you find your time here worth the visit!
