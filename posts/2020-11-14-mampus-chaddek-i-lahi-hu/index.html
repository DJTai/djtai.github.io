Mampus Chaddek i Lahi-hu

2020-11-14
blogs
translation provided

Kada diha, hu li’i' malålagu i lahi-hu gi uriyan guma'-måmi. Gaigi gui' gi me’nan potta, pues ha malågu hålum i kuatton-måmi, pues gaigi gui' gi tatti yu' pat fi’on-hu. Ai adai, guaha na biåhi ti siña hu sodda' i tiempu para dumeskånsa.

Lao gi minagåhet, eyu ha na’magof yu' sa' hu lili’i' gui' dumångkulu; mås chumachålek gui', gof ya-ña humugågandu yan hami yan si Lorie asaguå-hu, ya bula kumukuentus gui'!

Ti siña hu nangga para lumi’i' håfa guaha na umeyak gui'.

Todu ha' para på’gu // Bula guinaiya — DJT.

English Translation

My Son Is Too Fast

Every day, I see my son running around our house. He’s at the front door, then he runs into the room, then he’s behind me or right next to me. My goodness, sometimes I can’t find the time to relax.

But honestly, it makes me happy because I’m seeing him grow; he’s laughing more, he really likes playing with me and my wife, and he’s talking so much!

I can’t wait to see what else he learns.

That’s it for now // Much love — DJT
