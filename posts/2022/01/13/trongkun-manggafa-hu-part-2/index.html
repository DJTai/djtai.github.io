First things first: if you haven’t seen my first post re: my family tree then you can read it here.

Okay, nihi ta tutuhun: I’ve been busy, dispensa yu'! I’ve been neck-deep in work and life in general, but I’m here now and I’m excited to share an update on my family tree! 🙌🏽 I wasn’t sure how much further I’d get honestly - I was doing so much research prior to December that I felt I’d found all the info that I could. Then I subscribed to https://www.chamorroroots.com/v7/, Siñot Punzalan’s geneaology website, and the excitement came rushing back! I was able to find 21 new people to add to my tree 😳! And while not as impressive a number, my aunt gave me some documents that gave me 4 more people, and also birth/death years for others who had that data missing 👏🏽. Needless to say: sen nina’magof yu' nu esti!

Not much else to say but to give updates.

I Trongkun Manggafå-hu På'gu

Note: the colors represent their age at death, and it’s on a sort of gradient. The range is as follows:

The outer white boxes mean I don’t have an age - I either don’t have a birthdate or a deathdate.

I Bandan Tatå-hu

  • Two greatx2 grandparents (on his mom’s side)

Pues Håfa På'gu?

For now I’ll try and start filling out the siblings I guess 😅? I spent most of my time only focused on the parents, grandparents, etc. Also, knowing that Siñot Punzalan’s database exist, it’d probably be best to start getting info on folks I know are not in his dataset, e.g., my cousins, my nieces and nephews, etc. Also, I have to start getting more info gi bandan asaguå-hu lokkui'! Gof åpurao yu' put ennao.

Todu ha' para på’gu. Bula guinaiya! — DJT.
