Hu Fa'mamaolek Esti na Blog

I'm Fixing This Blog

By David Taitingfong | June 13, 2021 | 1 minutes
todu chamoru

Håfa adai todus hamyu guennao huyung! Hu tungu' na tumaigui yu' giya guini, lao hu prometi hamyu na ti åpmam bai tutuhun månggi' guini. Buenti gumuaha tiempo-ku gi mamaila' na semåna, lao siempri gi mamaila' na mes - puedi ti mås anåkku' sa' malago' yu' fa’maolek esti na blog. Bai hu tulaika i “theme” ya hu tungu' na gumuaha meggai tinilaika siha ni' hu chek pat chagi.

Todu ha' para på’gu. Bula guinaiya! — DJT.

English Translation

Hey everyone out there! I know that I’ve been absent, but I promise y’all that I’ll start writing soon. Maybe I’ll have time in the coming week, but surely I’ll have time in the coming month - hopefully not longer because I want to fix this blog. I’m going to change the theme and I know there’s gonna be plenty to check and test.

That’s all for now. Much love! — DJT.
