Listan Tiningu’

A list of Chamoru resources that I frequent:

Resource Notes
Learning Chamoru Chamoru dictionary & lessons. Account required.
Peter Onedera’s PDN Articles Siñot Onedera writes columns for the Guam PDN - every articles is in Chamoru & English!
Chamorro Grammar by Sandra Chung. Downloadable PDF.
Minagåhet Blog by Michael Lujan Bevacqua. General Chamoru knowledge.
Påle’ Eric’s Blog by Påle’ Eric. Gi minagåhet, his blog is probably what I’m aiming for in terms of knowledge base.
Lengguahi-ta by Schyuler; blog to document her Chamoru language learning journey
Trini Learns Chamorro by Trini; their blog to actively practice Chamoru
The Salty Chamoru Instagram mini-blog with a new phrase almost every day. Account required.
