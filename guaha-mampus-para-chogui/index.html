Guaha Mampus Para Cho'gui

There's Too Much To Do

By David Taitingfong | January 27, 2021 | 1 minutes
todu chamoru

Ai adai, dispensa na gof atrasao ini na blog! Gi minagåhet, sen tinani' yu' lao guaha maolek na rason na taiguini. Na’kadada' un anåkku' na istoria: ti apmam bai in fañåga gi nuebun guma', yan i mañainå-hu yan che’lu-hu. Ohala' mohon na ti apmam siña månggi' yu' kosa ki siña hu sangåni hamyu håfa nuebu gi lina’lå'-hu. Esta otro tiempu nai!

Todu ha' para på’gu. Bula guinaiya! — DJT.

English Translation

My goodness, apologies for this late blog! Truthfully, I’m super busy but there’s a good reason for that. Long story short: I’m moving soon into a new house, along with my parents and sister. Hopefully I can write soon so I can give an update on life. Until next time!

That’s all for now. Much love! — DJT.
